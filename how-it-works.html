How MeshRFID works

Simple line from tag to balance sheet. You tag, scan and MeshRFID does the rest – no heavy IT project.

Step 1
Tag inventory as it arrives

We supply encoded RFID tags matched to your SKUs. As goods arrive, staff apply tags during normal intake. No extra headcount, no new processes to learn.

Step 2
Walk and scan

Use a handheld reader to walk the shop floor or warehouse aisles. MeshRFID captures every item in range and reconciles it with your catalogue.

Step 3
Dashboards for ops and finance

Real-time views show what is actually on hand versus what the system thinks is there, with shrink, ageing and mis-location surfaced for operations and finance teams.

Step 4
Prove ROI

Track the value of stock brought back into sale, shrink reduction and time saved on stock counts. Export clean data for your finance system or auditors at any time.
