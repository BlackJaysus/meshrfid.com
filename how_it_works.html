How MeshRFID works

Built for finance and operations teams who need a single view of stock. From sole traders to global warehouse networks, MeshRFID links item, value and location into one real-time picture.

The three-step flow

①

Tag what matters

Apply RFID at the point that suits your business – at inbound, in store, or at the 3PL. Start with low-cost labels and mobile or handheld readers, add fixed infrastructure later.

②

Capture movement in real time

Reads from phones, handhelds and fixed antennas are translated into item-level movements across stores, warehouses and staging areas. No manual spreadsheets, no guesswork.

③

Control stock and value

MeshRFID links quantities, locations and costs so finance and operations see the same numbers – shrink, mis-ships, aged stock and working capital tied up in inventory.

Start with a single site and a phone. Add handhelds, portals and dock-door readers as the business and ROI demands – the software layer stays the same.
