Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

This page explains how MeshRFID (“we”, “us”) handles information collected through this website.

Information we collect

  • Details you enter into forms (name, email, store name, message).
  • Basic technical information (such as browser type, device type and approximate region) via analytics, if enabled.

How we use this information

  • To contact you about demos, onboarding and support.
  • To understand demand and improve the product.

How we store and protect data

Form submissions may be stored in our CRM tools and in our database provider (for example, Supabase). We limit access to this data to people working on MeshRFID and take reasonable steps to keep it secure.

Sharing your data

We do not sell your data. We may share it with service providers who help us operate the product (for example, hosting and email providers), under appropriate data protection terms.

Your rights

You can ask us to update or delete your information by emailing info@meshrfid.com.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact info@meshrfid.com.
