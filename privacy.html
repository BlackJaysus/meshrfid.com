Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

This page explains how MeshRFID (“we”) handles information collected through this website.

What we collect

  • Details you submit in forms (name, email, store name, message).
  • Basic technical data such as browser type and approximate region via analytics tools, if enabled.

How we use it

  • To reply to enquiries, demos and onboarding requests.
  • To understand demand and improve the product.

Storage and security

Form data may be stored in our email and database providers. Access is limited to people working on MeshRFID. We take reasonable steps to keep data secure.

Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share it with service providers who help us run the service (for example hosting and email), under appropriate data protection terms.

Your choices

You can ask us to update or delete your information by emailing info@meshrfid.com.
