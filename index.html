FAQ

A few common questions from finance, ops and IT teams. If you need more detail, just reach out.

Is pricing based on SKUs or individual items?

Pricing is based on the number of active tagged items in circulation, not the number of SKUs. A size run of the same style still counts as multiple items because each unit has its own tag.

Can we start on one plan and move up later?

Yes. You can upgrade from Starter to Standard or Pro at any time as volumes grow or you need more finance features. Your data and history stay in place.

What does ‚Äúwalk and scan‚Äù actually do today?

The handheld reader captures every tag in range and MeshRFID compares that count with your expected stock for that location. You see variances and unknown tags, so you can investigate shrink or misplacements quickly.

Do we need new hardware?

You can bring your own compatible handheld readers, or we can supply hardware bundles on Standard, Pro and Enterprise plans. Hardware is priced separately from the software subscription.

How do integrations work on Pro?

Pro exposes APIs and connectors so you can feed counts and valuations into ERP or finance systems. Typical integrations push location-level counts, variances and ageing into your existing reporting stack.
