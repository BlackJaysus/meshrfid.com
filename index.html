FAQ

Is the first month really £1 on all non-Enterprise plans? +
Yes. Starter, Grow and Pro are £1 for the first month per organisation. After that, standard pricing applies at the rate shown for your chosen billing option.
What’s the difference between Grow and Pro? +
Grow is for small teams and regional networks – up to roughly 20,000 active tagged items across locations with scheduled finance exports. Pro is built for chains, warehouses and 3PLs that need unlimited tags, multi-currency reporting, ageing analysis and direct ERP / finance integrations.
Are tag volumes measured by SKU or by individual item? +
Volumes are based on active tagged items, not SKUs. If you have 200 units of the same SKU, that counts as 200 active tagged items.
Can I switch plans later? +
Yes. You can move between Starter, Grow and Pro as your volumes and requirements change. Enterprise plans are agreed on a 1–3 year term.
