How it works

Roll out RFID with a lightweight stack that connects stores, warehouses and finance in a single view.

Step 1

Tag and connect your inventory

We help you tag existing and inbound stock, map locations and define the views finance and ops need.

Step 2

Scan and sync

Teams use handheld readers to capture movements. MeshRFID syncs counts, transfers and shrink in real time.

Step 3

Report and optimise

Dashboards and exports feed into your existing tools so you can reduce loss, rebalance stock and prove ROI.
