Frequently asked questions

A quick overview of how billing, hardware and rollout work with MeshRFID.

Can I switch plans later?

⌄
Yes. You can move between Starter, Grow and Pro as your volumes change. Enterprise programmes are scoped individually but can also be re-baselined as you scale.

Is hardware included?

⌄
Starter assumes you bring your own compatible handheld reader. Grow, Pro and Enterprise can be bundled with readers and tags, priced per rollout.

How does the £1 first month work?

⌄
On eligible plans we charge £1 for the first month while you install, tag and learn the workflow. Standard rates apply from month two.

Does “reconciles with your catalogue” sit in the MVP?

⌄
Yes. MeshRFID maps tag reads back to your product catalogue via SKU or barcode. The MVP focuses on one-way reconciliation into MeshRFID with CSV/API syncs, then exposes finance-ready views. Two-way catalogue updates into your ERP can be added for Pro and Enterprise.
