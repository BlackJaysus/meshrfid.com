FAQ

A few common questions from finance, ops and IT teams. If you need more detail, just reach out.

Is pricing based on SKUs or individual items?

Pricing is based on the number of active tagged items in circulation, not the number of SKUs. A size run of the same style still counts as multiple items because each unit has its own tag.

Can we start on one plan and move up later?

Yes. You can upgrade from Starter to Grow or Pro at any time as volumes grow or you need more finance features. Your data and history stay in place.

What does “walk and scan” actually do today?

The handheld reader captures every tag in range and MeshRFID compares that count with your expected stock for that location. You see variances and unknown tags, so you can investigate shrink or misplacements quickly.

Do we need new hardware?

You can bring your own compatible handheld readers, or we can supply hardware bundles on Grow, Pro and Enterprise plans. Hardware is priced separately from the software subscription.

How does support differ by plan?

Starter is self-serve with onboarding guides and templates. Grow adds email support, while Pro and Enterprise add priority response times and scheduled success reviews. Enterprise customers also receive a named account team.
