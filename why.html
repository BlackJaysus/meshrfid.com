Why RFID needs a reset

RFID that doesn’t need a big budget or a big team.

Most RFID programmes are built around heavy hardware, long projects and specialist people. MeshRFID is the opposite – straightforward to run, low on capex and usable by anyone with a smartphone.

  • Runs on phones and simple readers instead of racks of new terminals.
  • Fits around the systems you already use – WMS, ERP, spreadsheets – rather than replacing them.
  • Shows stock, value and ageing in plain language that operators and finance can both act on.
Start with one site. If it pays for itself, roll it out.

What you actually get with MeshRFID

The goal is simple: reliable stock and valuation data that anyone can use, without turning RFID into a science project.

Cost
Low capital, fast payback

Start with existing devices and a focused scope. Track shrink and stock accuracy so you can see the impact in months, not years.

Simplicity
Built for busy floors, not labs

Clear tasks, guided counts and simple views. If someone can use a modern app on their phone, they can run MeshRFID.

Stock intelligence
Numbers people can make decisions on

Live view of what you own, where it sits and how long it has been there – by site, client or channel – ready for finance, ops and leadership.
